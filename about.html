About Tech Talent South

Tech Talent South (also known as TTS) is an Atlanta-based company dedicated to fostering talent in technology throughout the southeast United States (and beyond!) and transforming the entire region into a high-tech hub during the process. In order to make this dream a reality, Tech Talent South has placed campuses in high-growth technology areas such as Asheville, Atlanta, Charlotte, Raleigh and soon to be Miami and New Orleans. These campuses are located in the heart of the tech scene in each respective community and provide students with great opportunities to connect with local tech talent.

In fact, TTS emphasizes community and support through a diverse series of collaborative educational and networking events that bring together local startup/tech companies and local professionals. We also offer results-focused mentoring that really enables students to bring their own ideas to life and actually create business plans.

Our coding courses feature the most practical and widely used languages, as well as the insanely popular Internet application framework Ruby on Rails, which has been utilized in creating tools on sites such as Hulu and Twitter. While going through the course, students also learn the uses of HTML/CSS and interactivity-enhancing JavaScript in creating websites that will create a superior web experience with their application. The Full-time Code Immersion Class meets four mornings a week and includes office hours with instructors and the all-day use of shared working spaces. On the flip side, the part-time class meets two nights a week and is perfect for those currently with jobs.



Courses Tech Talent South offers:

Code Immersion Program:

Tech Talent South’s Code Immersion program is a two month bootcamp to teach you how to code. Whether you have some knowledge in the field or don’t know how to open a browser, we will give you the skills to code, design, and produce web applications. Make your application dreams come true by learning Ruby on Rails, domain modeling, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, AJAX, and much more in this eight week course.

Game Development Class:

If you know a little bit about coding, love playing games, and dream of building your own, Tech Talent South’s Game Development Class is exactly what you need! In this two weekend class you’ll learn fundamentals in game development and will go from being a beginner to being able to publish your own games.

Youth Camps:

There’s no doubt the best coders are yet to come. Give your kids a leg up and enter them in one of our one-day youth camps! We’ll teach them the basics in web development, including languages such as Ruby and HTML, as well as how to develop an idea and show what is possible with technology.

College Cram Session:

Tech Talent South’s 10-day College Cram Session is perfect for those current or recent graduates who are looking for the basics in web development. You’ll learn the fundamentals of how to create a functional prototype and help you achieve your personal programming goals.
